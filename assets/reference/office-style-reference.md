# Agent OS Office Style Reference

Use the attached office image as style and layout inspiration, not as a direct copy.

## Visual Direction

- Bright, crisp pixel art office RPG scene with a top-down three-quarter view.
- Blue-gray carpet tile floor with clear walking lanes between work zones.
- Large upper window wall with a simple city skyline outside.
- Modular game background: furniture, boards, desks, portal, lounge, and service desk should read as separable props for animation and swapping.
- Professional but playful tech-office mood, colorful enough for a game UI while staying readable behind sprites and overlays.

## Layout Anchors

- Task board near the upper-left or upper-center wall.
- Generic Agent OS wall brand or four-color square motif near the task board; avoid real company logos or trademarked marks.
- Vendor or temporary-agent portal in the upper-right corner.
- Outsource service desk in the lower-left area with a small help monitor or robot-like terminal.
- Five to six employee desks across the middle of the office.
- Secretary station near the boss-facing control area.
- Rest area with sofa, coffee table, and relaxed props in the lower-center or lower-right.
- Supporting decorations: plants, boxes, water cooler, vending/server machine, small shelves, cable details, and soft window light.

## Asset Constraints

- No exact reproduction of the reference image.
- No Microsoft logo, third-party trademarks, watermarks, or readable text labels.
- Keep silhouettes clear for gameplay collision zones and sprite movement.
- Favor separated object clusters and stable perspective so assets can be layered, moved, or animated.
